Dataset Description
COVID-19 Patient Symptoms & Diagnosis Dataset
📊 Dataset Overview
Total Records: ~1,500 patients
Total Features: 18 columns
Target Variable: covid_result (Binary Classification)
* Format: CSV

🧾 Feature Categories
Demographics: Age, Gender
Symptoms: Fever, Dry Cough, Fatigue, Headache, Shortness of Breath, Loss of Smell, Loss of Taste, Chest Pain
Clinical Indicators: Oxygen Level, Body Temperature
The datasets you can find here contain a training data (train.csv) and a test data (test.csv). Your submitted prediction for the test.csv should have the same format as the sample_submission.csv.
